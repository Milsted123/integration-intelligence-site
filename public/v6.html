Why integrations fail

Four priorities. In every successful integration.

01
Tie strategy to execution

The deal thesis is clear. How to execute against it usually isn't. Align the integration to the outcomes — every workstream must earn its place.

02
Maintain momentum

Lose it and you lose everything. Without structure and accountability from day one, integrations stall — and rarely recover.

03
Focus on people

Things go wrong — they can usually be recovered. Losing the people you need cannot. A proactive talent strategy is where integrations are quietly won or lost.

04
Govern from the start

Decision rights, governance, synergy tracking — unglamorous but essential. Uncertainty destroys value. Build the structure before you need it.
